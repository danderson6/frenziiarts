Digital Afro


Creative Director: David Anderson
Concept and Design by David Anderson

The idea behind Digital Afro is to produced quality products on different mediums from t-shirts, stickers, poster, custom products and much more. We are open to collaborating with others designers, artists and organizations to produced quality ,unique, limited items that we want to see in our environment. To bring back the old school analogue feel to a digital world. Another goal is to donate a percentage to non-profit organizations in Montreal. We feel that it is important as artists to give back to the community and the world.
