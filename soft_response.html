Soft Response


Artists Collaboration David Anderson and Slavica Panic

“Soft response” is a two part installation, based on QR code*. It uses a generated pattern and transforms it into three dimensional objects, while preserving the main function - providing information. “Soft response” consists of two pieces: wall piece (8x8ft) and floor piece (10x10ft). The wall piece uses a pattern from the website specially created for the show, whose content will change daily. The audience and press will have the opportunity to explore an art piece in a new level. By scanning the piece with their cell phones, visitors will be taken to the website and watch a different videos related to art and fashion, every day during the show. The floor piece uses the pattern generated from artists’ website, and it’s a place where visitors can sit and relax.
